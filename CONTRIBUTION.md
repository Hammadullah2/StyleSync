## [cite_start]Branch Naming Convention [cite: 64]
All branches should follow this pattern:
* `feat/<feature-name>`: (e.g., `feat/fastapi-predict-endpoint`)
* `fix/<bug-name>`: (e.g., `fix/docker-build-error`)
* `docs/<doc-update>`: (e.g., `docs/update-readme`)
* `infra/<infrastructure-change>`: (e.g., `infra/setup-mlflow-tracking`)
